About

I'm passionate about using technology to help mission-driven organizations make a real difference in people's lives. I work with public sector and nonprofit teams to transform outdated systems into modern, user-friendly tools that actually work for the people who need them most. Whether it's helping a government agency serve citizens more effectively, streamlining processes so staff can focus on their core mission, or building accessible platforms that connect communities, I believe technology should solve real problems and create meaningful impact. My experience spans modernizing legacy systems, automating tedious processes, and creating digital solutions that help dedicated public servants and mission-driven teams accomplish their goals and better serve their communities.

Chicago Affordable Housing App

The Chicago Affordable Housing App provides a current listing of affordable housing in Chicago and Illinois maintained by the City of Chicago, aggregated from the City of Chicago's data portal. It provides a Google map of housing locations, filtered location listings by community and other property types, the ability to save a list of favorite locations, and the ability to maintain notes about locations.
